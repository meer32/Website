About Myself

An A Level student living and working on the Isle of Wight. A passion for cinematography and live video performance.
I have a passion for creativity through moving image. The enjoyment of capturing a story on film and editing the piece into a final form provides excitement and exploration into if an idea works. Alongside this technical working environments with teams sparks new ways to capture an idea and provide ways to relay it to a final audience.
Media production through the use of Television and film allows for the exploration of people's imagination and allows for a sense of escapism. Being able to transport people through the use of lighting, camera and sound provide me with a sense of challenge but enjoyment. Video editing and Camera operation are my areas which excite me the most due to being able to truly develop the production both before and after post-production.
